About Us

The Mandate
To raise a generation that will spark and revive the spirit of Godliness in every facet of life. We long for a generation sold out to God by REVIVAL .
Who We Are
TCCI Global is a dynamic tribe raised to develop transformational leaders who will impact systems, structures, and industries for the Kingdom of God.
Our Vision
We at The Cephas Church International (TCCI) are burdened with a mandate to raise and equip Christ like believers with the aim of influencing and impacting our generation and those coming behind. As a movement WE are to be grounded in sound teaching of the praticalize/radicalize word of TRUTH, the complete expression and display of heaven’s authority and the proclaimed dominion over the works of the devil throughout all ages, race, social & corporate stratosphere.
Our Mission
To redeem lost souls while redirecting their focus to the true nature and intent of God Almighty, without an iota of condemnation in our approach. We simply are on a rampage to unveil the TRUE image of Jesus Christ to MANKIND.
Core Values
Prayer, Worship, Word(Bible)

Leadership/Pastors

  1. Pastor Gbenga O. Ajana

    Pastor Gbenga Ajana is the Lead Pastor of Cephas Church International, a passionate teacher, life coach, and mentor dedicated to raising purpose-driven individuals. With a background as a security expert, he brings wisdom, discipline, and clarity to both spiritual and practical life matters.

  2. Minister Olawunmmi Ajana

    Minister Olawummi Ajana serves as the "Mother of the House," bringing warmth and wisdom to our ministry. A pillar of support and a compassionate mentor, she is dedicated to building a church family rooted in love and hospitality. Whether coordinating behind the scenes or offering heartfelt counsel, she works alongside the Senior Pastor to ensure every member feels seen, valued, and restored through the grace of God.

  3. Minister Akolawole Omobaba

    Minister Ayodele Kolawole Damilola serves as our Associate Pastor, blending spiritual fervor with professional excellence. As a gifted intercessor and worship leader, he uses the "gift of sound" to draw souls closer to God. Beyond the altar, Ayodele applies his engineering background and technical expertise to lead our church operations and digital growth. He is a songwriter and reader dedicated to serving the kingdom through prayer and technology.

  4. Minister Matthew Adebare (Apostle Edit)

    Minister Matthew Adebare is a Computer Science graduate who serves as our lead digital designer and a powerful teacher of the Word. Known for his strength as a prayer warrior, he bridges the gap between technology and faith. He is dedicated to sharing the Gospel through both visual excellence and deep scriptural insight, helping our community grow closer to God in this digital age.

  5. Minister Bukolamide

    Min. Kegbyale Bukola is one of the vocal powerhouses of the ministry, leading with grace and anointing. Her voice creates an atmosphere that ushers hearts into deep worship. She is also a professional background vocalist and a talented photographer.
